Do you have the full historical curve at date t?
│
├── YES (IR_RATE, IR_VOL surface, FX_VOL smile)
│     → Scenario A: interpolate RF(t, τ-1) directly
│       Most accurate. Use for all curve-based asset classes.
│
├── PARTIAL (you have theta from your model but not full curve)
│     → Scenario B: use BS theta / sqrt(T) approximation
│       Good for EQ_VOL, FX_VOL single points, IR_VOL ATM
│
└── NO (only time series, no curve — CP_PRICE, COMM_PRICE, EQ_PRICE)
      → Scenario C: rolling median as carry estimator
        Robust, works on any series, but carry is estimated not exact
        Use window=60d for stable estimate, tighten to 20d in fast regimes
